### Project Title: Digital Campaign for a Local Nonprofit Organization

#### Project Overview:
The team will create a comprehensive digital marketing campaign for a local nonprofit organization. This campaign will include a website, promotional videos, graphics for social media, and a detailed marketing strategy. Each member will contribute their expertise to deliver a cohesive project.

#### Objectives:
- Raise awareness for the nonprofit's mission and initiatives.
- Increase community engagement and volunteer sign-ups.
- Create shareable content to promote online visibility.

### Team Roles and Responsibilities:

1. Digital Marketing Student:
   - Responsibilities:
     - Develop a marketing strategy, including target audience analysis and campaign goals.
     - Plan and schedule social media posts.
     - Analyze campaign performance metrics.
   - Deliverables:
     - Marketing strategy document.
     - Social media calendar.

2. Web Developer:
   - Responsibilities:
     - Design and build a user-friendly website for the nonprofit.
     - Ensure mobile responsiveness and SEO optimization.
     - Integrate donation functionality.
   - Deliverables:
     - Fully functional website.
     - User experience report.

3. Filmmaker:
   - Responsibilities:
     - Create promotional videos highlighting the nonprofit’s mission, events, and community impact.
     - Film interviews with volunteers and beneficiaries.
     - Edit and produce the final video content.
   - Deliverables:
     - Two promotional videos (1-2 minutes each).
     - Behind-the-scenes footage for social media.

4. Graphic Designer:
   - Responsibilities:
     - Design graphics for social media posts, website banners, and promotional materials.
     - Create a cohesive visual identity that aligns with the nonprofit's branding.
   - Deliverables:
     - Set of social media graphics (10-15 posts).
     - Website graphics and banners.

### Project Timeline:

| Week | Tasks |
|------|-------|
| 1 | Initial meeting to brainstorm ideas and assign roles. Research the nonprofit and define campaign goals. |
| 2 | Digital Marketing: Develop strategy. Web Developer: Create wireframes. Filmmaker: Outline video concepts. Graphic Designer: Begin visual identity. |
| 3 | Web Developer: Build website. Filmmaker: Start filming. Graphic Designer: Design social media graphics. |
| 4 | Complete website, video production, and graphics. Begin integrating all components. |
| 5 | Finalize all deliverables. Prepare for presentation. Launch the campaign online. |

### Deliverables:
1. Website - A fully functional, responsive website for the nonprofit.
2. Marketing Strategy Document - A detailed strategy outlining goals, audience, and KPIs.
3. Promotional Videos - Two engaging videos ready for online platforms.
4. Graphic Assets - A collection of visually appealing graphics for social media and promotional use.

### Evaluation Criteria:
- Creativity: Originality of the campaign ideas and designs.
- Cohesion: How well all elements (website, video, graphics) work together.
- Execution: Quality of the final products and adherence to deadlines.
- Impact: Potential effectiveness of the marketing strategy based on projected engagement and outreach.

### Presentation:
At the end of the project, the team will present their campaign to the nonprofit organization, showcasing each member’s contributions and demonstrating the campaign’s potential impact.
